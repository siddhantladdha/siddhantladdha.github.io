Android

  • Android is a privacy nightmare1. If you are competent/willing/have time to tinker with Android without getting frustrated have a look at Lineage OS and /e/ OS2.
  • If above is not possible there are lots of guides on internet on how to disable privacy invading actions, the only hardwork required is in finding what works for you3.
  • Try using apps from F-Droid instead of from Play Store, you will actually have a better experience and many developers are helpful in bug fixes and feature requests.
